The Architect

WE ARE BRUTALIST

Inspired by Le Corbusier's Unité d'Habitation and raw concrete (béton brut) of mid-century architecture, we translate these principles into digital space.

The Manifesto

1

Honesty in Structure

We reject false comfort of rounded corners and soft shadows. The web was built on rectangles, borders, and boxes. We expose these structures rather than hide them. Every div is a concrete block. Every border is a structural beam.

2

Function Over Decoration

If an element doesn't serve a purpose, it doesn't exist. No decorative gradients. No meaningless animations. Every pixel earns its place. The result is lightweight, fast, and impossible to ignore.

3

Contrast as Communication

Black and white. Red and yellow. Maximum contrast for maximum impact. We don't whisper—we shout. Hierarchy is established through scale and weight, not subtlety. If it's important, it's huge.
